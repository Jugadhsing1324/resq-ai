      SAFETY GUIDES

      Essential knowledge for the most common emergency situations.

      01
      🔥

      Fire Safety

      Know your exits. Stay low in smoke. Never use elevators. Alert others before calling.

      • Drop, cover, crawl below smoke
      • Feel doors before opening
      • Meet at a designated outdoor spot
      02
      🌊

      Flood Response

      Move to higher ground immediately. Avoid floodwaters — even shallow fast water is deadly.

      • Never drive through flooded roads
      • Disconnect electrical appliances
      • Keep emergency kit accessible
      03
      🌍

      Earthquake Safety

      Drop, Cover, Hold On. Stay inside until shaking stops. Avoid windows and shelves.

      • Get under a sturdy desk or table
      • Stay away from exterior walls
      • Expect aftershocks
      04
      ❤️

      CPR Basics

      If someone is unresponsive and not breathing, call 108 and start CPR immediately.

      • 30 compressions, 2 rescue breaths
      • Push hard and fast (100–120/min)
      • Continue until help arrives
      05
      ⚡

      Electric Shock

      Do NOT touch the person if they are still in contact with the electrical source. Cut the power first.

      • Switch off main power at breaker
      • Use dry wood/rubber to separate victim
      • Call 108 — always treat as serious
      06
      🩸

      Severe Bleeding

      Apply firm, direct pressure to stop bleeding. Do not remove the cloth once applied — add more on top.

      • Press hard with a clean cloth
      • Elevate the limb above heart level
      • Call 108 for deep or arterial wounds
      07
      🌡️

      Heatstroke

      Heatstroke is life-threatening. Move the person to shade, cool them down immediately, and call 108.

      • Apply ice packs to neck, armpits, groin
      • Fan them while misting with cool water
      • Do NOT give fluids if unconscious
      08
      🧪

      Chemical Exposure

      Evacuate area immediately. Do not induce vomiting for ingested chemicals. Fresh air is critical.

      • Flush skin/eyes with water for 15+ min
      • Remove contaminated clothing safely
      • Call Poison Control: 1800-116-117
